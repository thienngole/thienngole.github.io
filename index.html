ABOUT ME

ThienNgo Le - Computer Science Engineer - Orchids Hobbyist

HELLO, WELCOME TO THIENNGO'S PAGE!

Thien-Ngo Le is a young Computer Science Engineer. Thien-Ngo got his master's degree in Computer Science specializing in Cyber Security from one of the top best graduate engineering schools in the nation, Colorado School of Mines. He also graduated with the highest honors of his class, Summa Cum Laude, from Metropolitan State University of Denver with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

From a young age Thien-Ngo expressed a strong interest in Cybersecurity. This interest has led him to pursue his master's degree in Computer Science, focusing on Cybersecurity. Thien Ngo has worked on a variety of projects such as Investigation of an Orthogonal Encryption Technique for Deep Learning, Analyzing the Security of Companision Apps of Popular Smart Home Devices, Internet of Things Device Penetration Testing, Making crowd-working more efficient, ethical, fair and trustworthy. Thien-Ngo was a contributor of Findings from Analysis and Visualization of the New York City Housing and Vacancy Survey Data project that is presented at JSM 2019 conference in summer 2019.

Outside the computing world, Thien Ngo is a professional orchid hobblyst. He has got many awards for his orchids from American Orchid Society. Thien Ngo has successfully created and registered several new orchid hybrids to the world orchid list.
