ThienNgo Le - Computer Science Engineer - Orchids Hobbyist

HELLO, WELCOME TO THIENNGO'S HOUSE!

Thien Ngo Le is a young Computer Science Engineer who recently graduated with the higest honors of his class, Summa Cum Laude, from Metropolitan State University of Denver with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. From a young age Thien Ngo expressed a strong interest in Cybersecurity. This interest has led him to pursue his master degree in Computer Science, focusing on Cybersecurity. Currently he is a graduate student at Colorado School of Mines, one of the top US national engineering university. Thien Ngo has worked on a variety of projects including Pedestrian Detection, Appying Machine Learning to Agricultural funded by Math & Computer Science department of Metropolitan State University of Denver. In early 2019, Thien Ngo was a backend developer of On The Quad, an event scheduler application specifically designed for students in Auraria campus in Denver, Colorado. In summer 2019, Thien Ngo and his classmates attended the JSM 2019 conference and presented their findings from statistical analyses and visualizations of the New York City Housing data. Thien Ngo currently works as a research assistance at Colorado School of Mine. His research area is in Big Data Analytics and Cybersecurity.

Out side the computing world, Thien Ngo is a professional orchid hobblyst. He has got many awards for his orchids from American Orchid Society. Thien Ngo has successfully created and registered 6 new orchid hybrids to the world orchid list.
